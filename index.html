Hello!

I am an Assistant Professor at Texas Christian University's Department of Computer Science. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of North Texas, and bring a rich background in both academia and industry.

My research interests are in the broad areas of cybersecurity, AI, ML, wireless communication and networks, and emerging technologies such as 5G, 6G, and beyond networks. In addition to these areas, I am deeply passionate about the application of Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) techniques in network optimization and security.

I am looking for motivated undergraduate and graduate students interested in working in AI and ML for cybersecurity offense and defense. If you are interested and would like to work with me, please email me at robin.chataut@tcu.edu, including your resume.
