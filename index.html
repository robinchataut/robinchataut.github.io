Hello!

I am an Assistant Professor at Texas Christian University's Department of Computer Science. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of North Texas and bring a strong background in both academia and industry.

My research interests span cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, machine learning, wireless communication and networks, and emerging technologies including 5G, 6G, and beyond networks. I am particularly interested in applying AI and ML techniques to network optimization and security.

I am actively looking for motivated undergraduate and graduate students interested in working on AI and ML for cybersecurity offense and defense. If you are interested, please email me at robin.chataut@tcu.edu with your resume.
